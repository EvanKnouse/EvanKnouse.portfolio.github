Prj3 Beer Temperature Monitor

CST Year 2 Capstone Project

Public repository available at https://github.com/EvanKnouse/EvanKnouse.prj3beer.github. The app is designed to receive temperature information from a Bluetooth thermometer. The app will monitor your beer's temperature, and alert you when it reaches your desired temperature. As well, the app can store your favourite settings, share your experience and connect via Facebook, among many more features.

Application of Computed Tomography Techniques to Radiation Therapy

Physics 491 Capstone Project

The purpose of the capstone project was to investigate the application of computed tomography techniques for beam filtering to radiation therapy, using an idealized model. The paper outlines the general physics of radiation as it pertains to radiation therapy and computed tomography (CT), as well as the convolutional mathematics of beam filtering via the Radon transform.
